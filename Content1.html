3 Ways to Tie a Bowline Knot
  • Form a small loop by crossing the working end (the shorter end) over the standing part (the longer end) of the rope. The working end should go under the standing part.
  • Pass the working end up through the loop you just made. This is the start of the knot.
  • Bring the working end around the back of the standing part of the rope.
  • Pass the working end back down through the loop.
  • Pull on the working end while holding the standing part to tighten the knot. Ensure the knot is secure by pulling on both the working end and the standing part.
